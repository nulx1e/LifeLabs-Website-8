Basic Information of the Typhoon

Typhoon Pablo, known internationally as Bopha, was a strong tropical cyclone that hit the Philippines in December 2012. It was located in the vicinity of Malaybalay, Bukidnon on December 4, 2012, with maximum sustained winds of 160 kph and gustiness of 195 kph moving west at 20kph. The Tropical Rainfall Measurement Mission (TRMM) estimated that Bopha delivered over 240 mm of rainfall near the coast.
